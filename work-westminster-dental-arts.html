Westminster Dental Arts Group

GBP audit & growth proposal for a dental practice in Westminster, Colorado.

Home / Work / Westminster Dental Arts Group
GBP Audit Competitor Analysis Citation Building Canva Proposal Deck Westminster, CO
Overview

The Brief

This case file is a full technical audit of a dental practice's Google Business Profile in Westminster, Colorado. The goal was to identify exactly why the practice was losing visibility to nearby competitors in the Local Pack, and package the findings into a growth proposal a practice owner could act on immediately.

Process

The Approach

  • Full profile completeness check — categories, attributes, services, and business description.
  • Competitor gap analysis against the top-ranking Local Pack results for target keywords.
  • NAP consistency review across existing citations to catch conflicting or outdated listings.
  • Citation opportunity mapping — directories and niche sites competitors were listed on that this profile wasn't.
  • Review and reputation signal audit, plus a look at posting and photo activity.
  • Findings consolidated into a prioritized fix list, ordered by expected impact vs. effort.
Output

Deliverables

The findings were packaged into a growth proposal deck (built in Canva) that walks through the competitor gap, the prioritized fix list, and the citation opportunities — written so a non-technical practice owner could follow the reasoning and see the business case for each recommendation.

This audit is also featured as a portfolio case study on Upwork.
