How I work

Four steps, in order.

01

Audit

Map every ranking factor — profile completeness, categories, citations, reviews, on-page relevance — before touching anything.

02

Diagnose

Separate what's actually holding a listing back from what's just cosmetic. Most audits stop at symptoms; this is where the real work starts.

03

Fix

Prioritize by impact versus effort. Cheapest, highest-impact fixes first — not the flashiest ones.

04

Verify

Re-check rankings against the original audit baseline. If it didn't move the needle, it gets flagged and revisited — not quietly dropped.
