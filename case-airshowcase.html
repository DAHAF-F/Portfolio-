AirShowcase

A gesture-controlled product reveal experience, built to explore conversion-first thinking outside of search.

Hand Tracking Canvas / WebGL Experimental Build
View Live →

The Brief

Most of my work lives in Google Business Profiles and Local Packs — this one doesn't. AirShowcase is a gesture-controlled product reveal built to test the same underlying instinct that drives good SEO work: reduce friction, make the thing people came for immediately obvious, and get out of the way.

The Approach

  • Real-time hand-tracking, used to let a visitor "grab" and rotate a product with no mouse or touch input.
  • A canvas-based hologram effect layered on top to make the reveal feel physical rather than flat.
  • Built and deployed as a standalone experience on Vercel, with a Formspree-powered contact flow.

Why It's Here

It's a deliberate outlier in this list. Local SEO is about making sure the right business shows up first — this is about what happens once someone's attention actually lands. Same underlying question about what earns attention, answered in a completely different medium.
